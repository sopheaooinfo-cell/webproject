How to Enable Location Access

Follow these steps to enable location access for this website:

Google Chrome

1
Click the lock icon in the address bar
2
Find "Location" in the permissions list
3
Change from "Blocked" to "Allow"
4
Refresh the page and try again

Mozilla Firefox

1
Click the (i) icon in the address bar
2
Click "More Information"
3
Go to the "Permissions" tab
4
Find "Access your location" and change to "Allow"

Safari

1
Go to Safari > Preferences
2
Click the "Websites" tab
3
Select "Location" in the left sidebar
4
Find this website and change to "Allow"
